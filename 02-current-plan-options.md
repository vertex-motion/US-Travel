# Current Plan Options

## Planning Brief

- Canonical traveler intent and pace constraints live in `01-trip-purpose.md`.
- Working window: July 1-20, 2026, Sydney-to-Sydney, with 17 US hotel nights.
- Route theme: San Francisco, Yosemite, Pacific coast, Los Angeles, and a Las Vegas city-resort finale.
- Planning posture: Las Vegas is now part of the primary plan. Preserve the 20-day duration by shortening earlier California stays instead of extending the trip.
- Cost posture: Value-conscious family lodging capped at USD $250 per night remains binding. Las Vegas resort fees, parking fees, and attraction tickets must be included in quotes, not treated as incidental.
- Route constraint: Keep local sightseeing days to about 3-4 hours in the car. Intercity transfer days can be longer, up to 10-12 hours in the car, when the destination change is worth it.
- Assumptions: The family can enter the US under ESTA if eligible. Flights will be quoted as Sydney-San Francisco and Las Vegas-Sydney via a connecting US gateway, plus a fallback Los Angeles-Sydney return.
- Open decisions remain tracked in `08-open-questions.md`.

## Known Tradeoffs

- Las Vegas now uses three hotel nights that previously supported slower San Francisco, Monterey, and Santa Barbara pacing.
- Muir Woods moves out of the default itinerary. Keep it as a San Francisco weather or interest swap only.
- Big Sur becomes a short scenic reach from Monterey/Carmel, not a dedicated full day.
- Santa Barbara is now a two-night pause. Do not load it with several fixed activities.
- Las Vegas in mid-July needs a heat-first schedule: early outdoor windows, hotel pool/rest time, indoor attractions, and evening Strip walks.
- Re-check Highway 1 status the week before travel; current Caltrans data already shows one-way controls north of Big Sur and near Jamala Road in Santa Barbara County.
- Re-check Yosemite reservation rules before booking flights and again 30 days before arrival.
- Quote both LAS-Sydney via connection and LAX-Sydney direct-return fallbacks before paying nonrefundable deposits.
- Treat a confirmed one-room or suite setup for all four travelers and a USD $200-$250 nightly rate as lodging requirements before choosing a hotel brand.
