## Practical Notes

- **SoFi Stadium capacity:** ~70,240. Parking lots fill early; FIFA recommends the LA Metro (Crenshaw/K Line, Inglewood/Downtown Inglewood station) or pre-booked official lots. Match-day drive from Pasadena on surface streets and I-110 can add 30–60 min over normal travel time.
- **Pram/stroller:** SoFi is ADA-accessible with ramps and elevators; prams are permitted. Plan extra time for security and concourse entry.
- **Family suitability:** A noon kick-off with a toddler is manageable. The 13-year-old will likely enjoy the atmosphere. Match duration is ~2 hrs plus warm-up and half-time — plan ~3 hrs at the venue plus travel.
- **Teams unknown:** For Match 84 (Jul 2), the specific teams playing are not confirmed until Group H and Group J conclude on June 26–27. If neither team has strong fan support, resale prices may drop close to match day.
