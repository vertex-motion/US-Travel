# Medical / Travel Insurance Research

Research on emergency medical travel insurance for this trip. Scope: emergency
medical cover only — cost and what each policy includes for medical. Cancellation,
baggage, and other extras are out of scope per traveler request.

Date researched: 2026-06-11. All prices are indicative; confirm with a live quote.

## Why Medical Cover Matters Most Here

- US hospital costs are among the highest in the world: AUD $10,000–$50,000+ per day is possible, and an air evacuation can exceed USD $50,000.
- For the USA, target **unlimited or at least AUD $1 million** emergency medical cover, plus unlimited medical evacuation/repatriation.
- Children are covered **free** as dependents on a family policy with every Australian insurer below (both the 13- and 2-year-old qualify), so you insure two adults and the kids are added at no cost.

Notes on the table:
- "Unlimited" means no fixed dollar cap on reasonable emergency medical or evacuation costs — the strongest position for the USA.
- Indicative costs assume two 39-year-old adults, kids free, comprehensive tier, standard excess, no pre-existing conditions. Medical-only tiers (e.g. Fast Cover Medical Only) are the cheapest route if you genuinely want medical cover and nothing else.
- A non-zero excess (typically AUD $100–$200) lowers the premium; you can usually buy the excess down to $0 for more.

## What "Emergency Medical" Includes (Typical, USA Comprehensive)

- Overseas hospital and treatment costs for sudden illness or injury.
- Emergency medical evacuation and repatriation to Australia.
- Emergency overseas dental for pain relief.
- 24/7 emergency assistance hotline.
- Repatriation of remains.

## Recommendation

1. For the USA, prioritize **unlimited emergency medical + unlimited evacuation**. On that test, Southern Cross (SCTI) Comprehensive, Cover-More Comprehensive, Fast Cover Comprehensive, and 1Cover Comprehensive all qualify.
2. If you want medical cover only and minimal extras, **Fast Cover Medical Only** (unlimited medical/evacuation) is the most cost-targeted option.
3. Get three live quotes (e.g. Southern Cross, Fast Cover, Cover-More) for the exact dates and travelers; expect roughly **AUD $250–$450 medical-only** or **AUD $350–$700 comprehensive** for the whole family.
4. Buy soon after the trip is fully booked so cancellation cover (if you later decide you want it) starts; medical cover itself applies from the travel dates.
