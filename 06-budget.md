# Budget

## Budget Posture

Use this as a planning allowance, not a booked quote. All amounts are in USD unless noted. Convert to AUD when flights and hotels are quoted.

The largest budget risk is lodging. July is peak season in Yosemite, the California coast, Santa Barbara, Los Angeles, and Las Vegas resort properties. The hotel target is USD $200-$250 per night, with $250 per night as the maximum unless the family explicitly approves an exception. A confirmed single room or suite for all four travelers is the key cost-control requirement because two rooms are likely too expensive.

Las Vegas can look cheaper than the California coast on room rate alone. Compare total stay cost, including resort fees, taxes, parking, pool fees or cabana temptations, attraction tickets, and any extra-person charges.

## Cost Controls That Preserve Quality

- Keep San Francisco car-free to avoid parking charges and city driving.
- Book Yosemite lodging first; a poor location creates more driving and less park time.
- Prioritize hotels that can confirm one room or suite for all four travelers at USD $200-$250 per night before comparing brands or amenities.
- In Las Vegas, compare total stay cost with resort fees and parking included.
- Use the direct Monterey-to-Santa Barbara transfer to avoid a one-night midpoint hotel unless live road or tour timing makes the fallback worthwhile.
- Choose one Los Angeles base and avoid cross-city sightseeing on the same day.
- Choose only one Las Vegas paid attraction or show until live prices are known.
- Treat San Diego and Grand Canyon as extensions, not free add-ons.

## Quote Tasks

1. Quote flights for July 1 departure from Sydney to San Francisco and July 18 departure from Las Vegas to Sydney via a connecting gateway.
2. Compare the Las Vegas return against a Los Angeles-Sydney return before paying nonrefundable deposits.
3. Quote Yosemite lodging immediately for July 5-8.
4. Quote one-room family configurations in each base within the USD $200-$250 nightly target before committing to exact dates or hotel brands.
5. Quote Las Vegas hotels with resort fees, taxes, parking, room occupancy, pool access, and cancellation terms included.
6. Quote rental car SFO city pickup to LAS return for July 5-18, then compare against SFO pickup to LAX return if flight quotes favor Los Angeles.
7. Rebuild the budget with actual booking quotes before paying nonrefundable deposits.
